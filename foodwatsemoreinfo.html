Food waste across the globe

In the U.S, 40 percent of what we grow ends up in the garbage. Globally, food waste is rising to 50 percent as developing nations struggle with spoilage and Western nations simply toss edible food away. This causes a negative factor to the world because wasting food isn't an option. It must end today.
